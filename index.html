SQL Project – E-Commerce Sales Analysis

Converted Excel sales data into a relational MySQL database and performed analytical SQL queries to extract business insights.

Sample SQL Queries

-- Monthly Sales Trend
SELECT
  DATE_FORMAT(order_date,'%Y-%m') AS month,
  SUM(sales_amount) AS total_sales
FROM orders
GROUP BY month
ORDER BY month;
      
-- Top 5 Products by Revenue
SELECT
  product_name,
  SUM(sales_amount) AS revenue
FROM orders
GROUP BY product_name
ORDER BY revenue DESC
LIMIT 5;

Insights: Identified top products and seasonal sales trends.
